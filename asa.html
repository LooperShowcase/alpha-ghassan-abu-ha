Legendary Camaro Models

Camaro ZL1 (Track Beast)

Engine: 6.2L Supercharged V8

Power: 650 HP, 650 lb-ft torque

0-60 mph: ~3.5 seconds

Top Speed: 198 mph

Special Features: High-performance Magnetic Ride Control, aerodynamic upgrades, and track-focused suspension.

Why It's Special: The most powerful Camaro ever, built for extreme performance on both the street and track.

Camaro SS (Classic Muscle)

Engine: 6.2L V8

Power: 455 HP, 455 lb-ft torque

0-60 mph: ~4.0 seconds

Top Speed: 165+ mph

Special Features: Brembo brakes, dual-mode exhaust, and a performance suspension.

Why It's Special: A perfect balance of power, style, and daily drivability.

Camaro IROC-Z (80s Icon)

Engine: 5.7L Tuned Port Injection V8

Power: 245 HP, 345 lb-ft torque

0-60 mph: ~6.5 seconds

Top Speed: 140+ mph

Special Features: Lowered suspension, performance-tuned shocks, and distinctive IROC-Z badging.

Why It's Special: One of the most iconic Camaros of the 1980s, named after the International Race of Champions (IROC).
