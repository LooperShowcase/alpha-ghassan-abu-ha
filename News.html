Camaro ZL1 Breaks Track Record at Nürburgring

March 15, 2025

The Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 1LE has set a new lap record for production muscle cars at the legendary Nürburgring Nordschleife, completing the 12.9-mile circuit in just 7:16.04. This impressive time places it among some of the most exotic supercars in the world.

The record-breaking run was achieved with the stock ZL1 1LE package, which includes the 650-horsepower supercharged V8, Multimatic DSSV dampers, and aggressive aerodynamics.

"This demonstrates the exceptional performance capabilities of the Camaro platform," said Alan Johnson, Chevrolet Performance Director. "The ZL1 1LE offers supercar performance at a fraction of the price."

Camaro Club Annual Meet Announced for Summer 2025

March 10, 2025

The National Camaro Club has announced its annual gathering will take place July 15-18, 2025, at the Indianapolis Motor Speedway. This year's event, "Camaro Fest XII," is expected to draw over 1,000 Camaros from all six generations.

Activities will include track days, autocross competition, a show-and-shine event, and technical seminars from Chevrolet engineers and performance specialists.

"This year marks the largest Camaro gathering in our club's history," said Maria Rodriguez, National Camaro Club President. "We're especially excited about the heritage display featuring rare COPO and Z/28 models from each generation."

Limited Edition Heritage Package Announced for 2025 Camaro

February 25, 2025

Chevrolet has announced a new Heritage Package for the 2025 Camaro lineup, celebrating the model's rich history with exclusive paint colors, graphics, and interior appointments inspired by iconic Camaros from the past.

The package will be available on SS and ZL1 models and includes five heritage-inspired colors: Hugger Orange, Rallye Green, Daytona Yellow, Riverside Blue, and Tuxedo Black. Each color comes with era-specific striping patterns and badging.

Inside, the Heritage Package features special embroidered headrests, a plaque showing the build number, and color-matched interior accents. Production will be limited to 1,967 units total (commemorating the first year of Camaro production).
